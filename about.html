Our Story

"Since 2021, we have translated the quiet glow of light into timeless pieces of gold."

Founded in the heart of Place Vendôme, Lumière Jewelry began as a private atelier for a select circle of clients. Our craft has grown into a modern expression of French luxury while staying rooted in the artisan’s bench.

Every piece is shaped with care, intention, and a deep respect for the gemstone. We create jewelry that feels personal today and lasting for years to come.

Ethical Sourcing

Every diamond and gemstone in our collection is traced from its point of origin. We strictly adhere to the Kimberley Process and prioritize recycled 18k gold.

Artisanal Excellence

Our master goldsmiths spend upwards of 200 hours on a single high-jewelry creation, ensuring perfection in every hidden detail.

Conflict-Free Promise

We partner exclusively with mines that meet the highest international labor and environmental standards.

Carbon Neutral by 2026

Lumière is on a dedicated path to achieving carbon neutrality across all ateliers and flagship boutiques by the end of 2026.
